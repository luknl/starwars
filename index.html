TATOOINE

Where everything begins.

" Tatooine was a world located in Arkanis area, unknown and distant in the Outer Rim world of Star Wars. Orbiting two suns, Tatoo 1 and 2, Tatooine was almost completely covered with arid deserts and swept by devastating sandstorms.

Despite of its isolation, Tatooine still the center of galactic importants events, including being the original World of Anakin Skywalker, Jedi knight who became Darth Vader, carrying with it the fall of the Old Republic and the beginning of Star Wars Saga. "
